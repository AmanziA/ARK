How ARK started

Our first ARK was on the 12th of December 2018 where we reached over 5000 people with acts of random kindness. The massive impact of ARK in 2018 led to the decision to execute ARK as an annual event to inspire people everywhere to show love and put smiles on the faces of others in their community.
